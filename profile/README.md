There are several types of IPTV content.

Video on Demand allows users to watch movies and TV shows whenever they want.

Unlike live television, VOD content is available anytime.

## Catch-Up TV

Catch-Up TV allows viewers to watch programs that aired previously.

This feature is useful for users who miss live broadcasts.

Time-shift television allows viewers to pause, rewind, or replay recently broadcast content.
